# Accessify — Product definition and positioning

## What Accessify is

**Accessify is a low-latency accessibility layer for physical live events.**

It is **not** a livestream platform. Accessify captures the **live audio signal** from the event and delivers **real-time accessibility services** to the audience. **It works even when the event has no livestream.** Participants access services through their **smartphones**.

---

## Core features

- **Ultra-low latency audio streaming** from the live event (capture of the live audio signal)
- **Real-time transcription** in the original language
- **Real-time translation** into multiple languages
- **Audio playback** of translated speech
- **Text captions** on mobile devices
- **Optional audio description** for visually impaired attendees
- **Optional sign language interpretation** when available at the event

---

## Positioning summary

| Do say | Don’t say |
|--------|-----------|
| Accessibility layer for physical live events | Livestream platform |
| Captures live audio from the event | Requires a livestream / streaming platform |
| Real-time captions and translation on smartphones | Inclusive streaming (as primary framing) |
| Works without a livestream | Depends on event having a stream |
| Low-latency accessibility services for in-person audiences | Online / hybrid-first product |

---

## Discovery and prioritization

Discovery and lead scoring **prioritize events where**:

- **Many languages are spoken** (multilingual environments)
- **Large international audiences** attend
- **Accessibility is important** (sector, mission, or regulation)
- **Public visibility is high** (open events, media, broad reach)

Livestream presence is **not** a requirement or a scoring factor. Physical live events with diverse, international, and accessibility-conscious audiences are the primary focus.
